## Practical Read

- Republican-leaning likely voters are the base plus soft/lean support. They are the GOTV and reinforcement universe.
- Likely persuasion targets are one group: lean-support and true-swing voters who are likely enough to vote after removing U/IT voters with Democratic/liberal evidence.
- Unaffiliated/Independent voters are either in the target universe or in the not-targeted U/IT audit. There is no separate reserve universe.
- Weak-D targets are registered Democrats who are likely to vote and have voter-level crossover evidence from L2, such as GOP primary history, Republican household, conservative donor/FEC, or clean conservative issue/context signals. Geography alone does not qualify them.
